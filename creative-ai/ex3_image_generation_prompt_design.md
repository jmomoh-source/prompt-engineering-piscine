**Prompt:**

Bold spray-paint textures with electric blues, hot pinks, and gold accents, capturing urban energy and rebellion.

## Comparison 
The first image I generated from a vague prompt, depicts a clean, aerial view of a utopian city with sleek glass towers and winding sky bridges, while the refined prompt produced a gritty, street level scene characterized by vibrant graffiti, neon textures, and a chaotic, rebellious atmosphere. 

This demonstrates that detailed, specific prompts give more creative control over style, perspective, mood, and visual elements, the refined prompt's explicit instructions about art style “graffiti”, viewpoint “street level”, and color palette electric blues, hot pinks, gold directly translated into the final image. I learned that vague prompts produce generic, default interpretations, while descriptive constraints unlock unique, tailored creative outputs.
