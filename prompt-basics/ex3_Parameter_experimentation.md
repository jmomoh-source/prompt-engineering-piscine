- Randomness: Temperature 0.9 showed more variation and creativity, temperature 0.2 was predictable and consistent.

### Top-p 0.5 vs Top-p 1.0:

### Overall Lesson:
Temperature mainly controls creativity and unpredictability, while top-p controls how wide the model’s word-selection pool is. Lower values produce focused, reliable responses, while higher values increase diversity and expressiveness. Choosing the right settings depends on whether the goal is precision or creativity.
