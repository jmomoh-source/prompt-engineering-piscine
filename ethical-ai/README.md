Part of the **Prompt Engineering Piscine** (Feb–Mar 2026).  
This quest focuses on building the right habits for lifelong learning — using AI to think deeper, not faster. The goal is to become irreplaceable by developing judgment, systems thinking, and a strong understanding of what you build.

---

## 📖 Overview
Before tackling bias or safety, this quest emphasizes *how to use AI to learn, not to skip learning*.  
Key themes:
- Using AI as a learning amplifier, not a shortcut  
- Practicing the workflow: attempt → verify → reflect  
- Building a personal code of ethics for AI use  
- Recognizing risks of over‑reliance on AI  
- Identifying human skills that make coders irreplaceable
