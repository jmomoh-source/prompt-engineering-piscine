---

## 📖 Overview
This quest goes beyond technical prompting to address the foundational question: *how should you use AI as a developer?*  
Key themes:
- Responsible AI principles: fairness, transparency, accountability  
- Identifying bias in datasets and outputs  
- Designing safe, clear prompts free from harmful assumptions  
- Handling sensitive or incorrect outputs responsibly  
- Using AI to learn, not to avoid learning
